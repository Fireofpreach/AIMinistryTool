Effective Sermon Structure
  1. Introduction - Engage the audience and introduce the topic
  2. Scripture Reading - Present the biblical text
  3. Main Points - Develop 3-4 key points from the text
  4. Illustrations - Use stories and examples to clarify
  5. Application - Connect the message to daily life
  6. Conclusion - Summarize and provide a call to action
Tips for Preparation
  • Start with prayer and biblical text
  • Focus on a single central idea
  • Use clear, accessible language
  • Include practical applications
  • Prepare more material than you'll need
