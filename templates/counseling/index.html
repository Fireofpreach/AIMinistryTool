Important Disclaimer
Pastoral Counseling Boundaries

This tool is designed to support pastoral counseling, not replace professional mental health services. Please observe these important guidelines:

  • Know when to refer to licensed professionals
  • Do not attempt to diagnose or treat clinical mental health conditions
  • Be aware of mandatory reporting requirements in your jurisdiction
  • Follow your denomination's pastoral counseling guidelines
  • Maintain appropriate boundaries and confidentiality

In cases involving abuse, self-harm, harm to others, or severe mental health issues, always refer to appropriate professionals immediately.
